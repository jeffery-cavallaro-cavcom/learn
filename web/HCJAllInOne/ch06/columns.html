The Cavallaro Family

The Cavallaro family (Jeffery, Leila, April, Peter, and Jay), located at 38712 Almaden Place, will be hosting Easter this year. In attendance will be Leila's parents, the Astarais, Leila's brother "Big Jay", and perhaps Leila's cousin Janet. Jeffery's parents live in Southern California and won't be making the trip this year. Of course, Ruca will be there looking for handouts. Tommie will also be there, but he will probably be hiding under the bed.

Interested parties should call the Cavallaros on their home number: 510-797-7712. Please call on Friday between the hours of 9am and 2pm if you would like to join the festivities.

Fry's is Fried

On a particularly sad note, the Fry's location in Fremont on the corner of Osgood and Auto Mall Parkway is no more. The company announced earlier this week that they were filing for Chapter 7 backruptcy and thus would shutter all remaining store immediately.

Electronics hobbyists will miss this once great source of components and test equipment. They will also miss the aisles of junk food approaching the registers.

First we lost Radio Shack, and now Fry's is gone as well.
